Bridging Data Science, Art, and Gaming

Hello, I'm Sraysohi, a dynamic professional with a passion for bridging the worlds of Data Science, Artistry, and Gaming.

Analytical Expertise

As a graduate student pursuing a Master's degree in Applied Statistics & Data Science at Jahangirnagar University, I've honed my skills in extracting meaningful insights from data. My academic journey has equipped me with a solid foundation in statistics, data analysis, and machine learning. 

Data Science by Day

In my professional life, I'm a Data Scientist, specializing in Python programming and frameworks like Pandas, NumPy, and TensorFlow. My experience spans from creating predictive models to conducting sentiment analysis and crafting interactive data visualizations using tools like Tableau. 

Artistic Expression

Beyond data analysis, I'm an artist at heart. In my free time, I immerse myself in the world of painting and sketching, allowing my creativity to flow onto the canvas. My art serves as an outlet for self-expression and a means to explore new perspectives.

Gaming Enthusiast

I also find joy in the realm of video games, where storytelling and interactive experiences merge. Gaming is not just a pastime for me; it's a source of inspiration and a reminder of the endless possibilities of creativity.

The Convergence

What truly sets me apart is my ability to seamlessly merge the precision of data science with the creativity of art and the immersive world of gaming. I see data as the foundation of compelling stories, and art and gaming as the mediums that bring those narratives to life. This fusion of skills enables me to create impactful, visually engaging solutions with a unique perspective.

Beyond Work

Outside of my professional pursuits, I'm deeply engaged in social entrepreneurship, with a rich history of volunteering and leadership roles at organizations such as YSSE. These experiences have not only broadened my horizons but have also instilled in me a commitment to making a positive impact on the world.
