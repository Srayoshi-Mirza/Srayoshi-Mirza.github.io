What is BKS?

BKS Pharma Consultancy is a Consultancy firm that works with small and medium-scale Allopathic, Uninaim Ayurvedic, Herbal, and homeopathic Medicine Manufacturing Companies. Their services are
► To prepare a project profile for setting up a new project /factory. ► The existing Lay Out Plan of the factory is to be renovated as per GMP Concept & In case of new infrastructure, Lay Out Plan is to be prepared as per GMP/ cGMP accordingly. ►To provide all assistance in implementing the recommendations given by the inspection team of DGDA. ► Documentation: Organogram, Job Description, Site Master File, Quality Manual, Quality Protocol, Validation Master Plan (VMP), All types of SOPs, Batch Manufacturing Record (BMR) , Batch Packaging Record (BPR), Test Criteria, Herbarium Sheet & provided all necessary types of documents ► Training : Training Schedule/ Calendar, Training Manual, Training Evaluation / Record including Providing all types of training
